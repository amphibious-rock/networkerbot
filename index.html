Networker Bot



Networker Bot, is a discord bot full of railway related commands, both fun and utility.

Popular commands include: /train-image /train-info /random-train-br /random-station. And the bot is not exclusive to British Railways either, with several commands relating to foreign railways too, based off contributions by the community, including countries such as Netherlands, Germany, Switzerland, and Denmark.
