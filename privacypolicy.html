Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18/02/26

Thank you for using Basic Bot. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our bot.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our bot:

  • Usernames
  • User IDs
  • Server the bot is used in
  • Any data you input into the bot
  • Messages and interactions with the bot

    • What is stored

  • Your username (In the leaderboard function)
  • How many commands you have executed (In the leaderboard Function)
  • Any text you input into the bot is outputted into the terminal, so can be viewed and should not be considered confidential. Terminal logs are routinely deleted.

    • You may request a copy of all your data stored.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect purely for the bot to run, we can only see what you enter into the bot. Specifically, we may use the interaction logs to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Analyse usage and improve functionality
  • Monitor and prevent abusive behavior

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and property

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access and use. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Your continued use of the bot after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new privacy policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at #help in the official discord server https://discord.gg/BM42aC2TaV .
